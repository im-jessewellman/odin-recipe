Baked Potato

Microwave baked potato in 10 minutes or less.

Ingredients:

  • Microwave
  • a potato

Steps:

  1. Step 1: place potato in Microwave.
  2. Step 2: set cook time on mircrowave to 8 minutes.
  3. Step 3: check at 8 minutes, add additional 1 minute until done.
  4. Step 4: Ready to eat.
