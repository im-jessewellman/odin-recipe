Minute Oatmeal

Just add hot water to minute oatmeal.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Mircowave or pot to boil the water
  • your instant oatmeal

Steps:

  1. Step 1: bring water to a boil.
  2. Step 2: Add hot water or add oatmeal to boiling water.
  3. Step 3: Let Minute oatmeal sit 1 minute in hot water until ready to eat.
