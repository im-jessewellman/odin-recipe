Minute Rice

Just add hot water Minute Rice.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Mircowave or pot to boil the water
  • your Minute Rice
  • a timer

Steps:

  1. Step 1: bring water to a boil.
  2. Step 2: Add hot water or add rice to boiling water.
  3. Step 3: Let Minute Rice sit covered for 5 minutes, after turning on low or off.
  4. Step 4: Set timer for 5 minutes or until ready to eat.
